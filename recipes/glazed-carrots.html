Glazed Carrots

Ingridients

  • Carrots Look for firm, plump, bright, and smooth carrots.
  • Butter You'll need about 1/4 cup butter to make these caramelized carrots.
  • Brown sugar You can use light or dark brown sugar and adjust the amount according to taste.
  • Seasonings These glazed carrots are simply seasoned with salt and ground white pepper.

Steps

  1. Cook the carrots
  2. Glaze the carrots
